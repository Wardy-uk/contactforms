Popup widget demo

Contact form popup widget, powered by BriefYourMarket.

This page demonstrates the contact form loading as a popup widget. The dialog is configured to appear on every visit (using afterVisits=1 and a non-expiring cookie setting).

  • Form markup is injected by Script > GetContactForm.
  • Popup behaviour is controlled by Script > DisplayContactForm.
  • Trigger timing controlled via afterVisits and expireAfterNumberOfDays.
